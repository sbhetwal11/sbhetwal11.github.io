I lead full-lifecycle control system delivery for GMP pharmaceutical and biotech manufacturing, from requirements and electrical design through PLC programming, SCADA, factory and site acceptance testing, commissioning, and validation under GAMP 5 change control, often across several regulated projects at once.

My focus is systems integration: connecting Rockwell PlantPAx and FactoryTalk, Inductive Automation's Ignition, OSIsoft/PI historians, MES, and SQL Server over OPC UA so that equipment, batch records, and enterprise data move as one system rather than isolated islands.

I hold an M.S. in Computer Science and a B.E. in Electrical & Computer Engineering. My research applies large language models and natural language processing to manufacturing and financial data, with a focus on on-premises, privacy-preserving systems for regulated environments. Outside of work, I am a hobbyist app developer and a vibe coder.
